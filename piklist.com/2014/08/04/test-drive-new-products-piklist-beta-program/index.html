Test drive our new products, with the Piklist Beta Program

Home » News » Test drive our new products, with the Piklist Beta Program

Aug 4
19

We need your help. With Piklist core well on its way to version 1.0, we are starting to roll out other plugins we have in the pipeline. Before making these products available to the general public, we are offering them to small groups of users for beta testing.

If you sign up, we would provide you with pre-release versions of our upcoming products. What do we want in return? Feedback. Lots of feedback. TONS of feedback. How useful are these products? What do you think of the workflow? The user interface? What features are we missing? Did you find any bugs?

These products are not production ready and SHOULD NOT BE USED IN A PRODUCTION ENVIRONMENT. We cannot stress that enough. Besides remedying the normally occurring bugs during this phase, we may also modify how things work, and how data is saved. Backwards compatibility is not our main focus while in beta; we want to maximize potential.

We are currently accepting beta signups for: “Piklist Toolbox” and “Piklist Fields and Forms”. Please watch our videos to learn more about them. Just a warning: turn down the volume before you start playing them… or turn it up ;)

You can sign up for our Beta Programs here >
