Required Featured Image when Publishing

You can now require a Featured Image when publishing a post. If there’s no image assigned, your post will be saved in Draft mode. Once you assign a Featured Image, you can Publish or Schedule.

List all Image sizes available

When you upload an image to WordPress, it automatically creates multiple versions based on your theme settings. Now you can see all the sizes available, complete with urls to each image.

Exif Data

If there’s Exif Data available for your images, WP Helpers will show it to you.

Image embed defaults

Click “Add Media”…  choose the image you want to place in your post.. and then choose Alignment, Link Type and Image Size. WP Helpers makes this easier by letting you set those defaults.

Over 50 more settings to customize your WordPress!

WordPress Helpers is your essential, one-stop plugin for taking control of WordPress.  Get it now at WordPress.org.
